  • Require one more parameter, which is direction, but is missing.

Graph Transition Matrix: Example: Reservoir's and Pumps Three reservoirs

Pumps move water from reservoirs A,B,CA, B, CA,B,C, using set linear equations. Can use matrix to express states of the reserviors over time(such as at the next day)

Graph transition matrix must be a square matrix, as only then will there be the notion of 'states', such as the amounts of water in A,B,CA,B,CA,B,C

Let the relevant square matrix representing the graph transition be MMM

Can augument MMM to create matrix MEMEME = [M E][M\space E][M E] ,and then gaussian elimination to reduce MMM to row echolon form. Whatever EEE (identity matrix) becomes then is the square inverse of matrix MMM, M−1M^{-1}M−1.
