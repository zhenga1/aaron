$W = f(x,y,z)$
Tangent hyperplane at $(x_0,y_0,z_0,f(x_0,y_0,z_0))$
$$W - f(x_0,y_0,z_0)=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} (x-x_0) +\frac{\partial f}{\partial y} (y-y_0) + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}(z-z_0)$$ Where$\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}$ are evaluated at point $(x_0,y_0,z_0)$.

Linearization is basically solving for $W$, in this case:
$$W = f(x_0,y_0,z_0)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} (x-x_0) +\frac{\partial f}{\partial y} (y-y_0) + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z}(z-z_0)$$
